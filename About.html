About This App

Purpose of the App

This app provides detailed information about heroes and villains from the Dragon Ball Z universe. It is designed for fans who want to explore character bios, power stats, and transformations in an interactive way.

Features

  • View character bios for Goku, Vegeta, Gohan, Broly, Piccolo, Freeza, and Gotens.
  • See detailed power stats and transformations.
  • Interactive hover boxes in the gallery to quickly view character info.
  • Floating bubbles in the background for visual effects.

How to Use the App

  1. Click on a character in the gallery to see detailed stats and images.
  2. Hover over character images to view quick info.
  3. Use the menu at the top to navigate between pages.

About Dragon Ball Z

Dragon Ball Z is a Japanese anime and manga series created by Akira Toriyama. It follows the adventures of Goku and his friends as they protect Earth and the universe from powerful villains. The series is known for epic battles, incredible transformations, and rich character development.
